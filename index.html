Experience.

I am a professionally trained guitar tech with over 15 years experience, local to the Dundee/Monroe area of Southeast Michigan. As a professional engineer and lifelong musician, I bring together my intimate knowledge of the instrument and attention to detail to make your instrument play like you've always wanted it to.

Quality.

We service all stringed instruments, with a specialty in electric and acoustic guitars and basses. This includes restrings, setups, hardware installation, electronics installation, fretwork, and more. Instruments are not one-size-fits-all, so we always consider your playing preferences for each setup to ensure it plays best in your hands.

Service.

We offer next-day service of restrings and setups for all electric or acoustic guitars, basses, and orchestral string instruments. While-you-wait service may also be available, allowing you to explore beautiful downtown Dundee or the local Cabela’s while your instrument is serviced. Pick-up and Drop-off service is also available within Monroe County for a mileage-based fee.

Upgrades.

Installation of new pickups, preamps, hardware, and custom assemblies are available. Please contact us before purchasing any new components, so we can ensure proper fit to your instrument.

Cables.

We also offer custom length guitar and patch cables. No cable length is too short or long, and we use the best available cable and connectors to ensure long–lasting life and signal integrity.
